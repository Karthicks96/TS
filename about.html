About Us

Get to know our team and our journey in IT support excellence

Our Story

Leading the way in IT support solutions

EService IT Solutions is a premier IT support provider based in Virudhachalam, Tamil Nadu. We specialize in delivering comprehensive hardware diagnostics, enterprise maintenance, and preventive IT solutions to businesses of all sizes.

With a team of 5 highly skilled engineers, we have been serving clients across Pondicherry, Villupuram, Cuddalore, Kallakurichi, and Chidambaram regions, building a reputation for excellence, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Our commitment to staying at the forefront of technology enables us to provide cutting-edge solutions that address the evolving needs of modern businesses.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth and development

2018

Foundation

EService IT Solutions was established in Virudhachalam with a mission to provide quality IT support services to local businesses.

2019

Service Expansion

Expanded our service portfolio to include enterprise maintenance and hardware diagnostics specializing in SMPS and motherboard troubleshooting.

2020

Regional Growth

Extended our service area to include Pondicherry, Villupuram, and Cuddalore, bringing our expertise to a wider client base.

2021

Team Expansion

Grew our team to 5 certified engineers to meet increasing demand and maintain our high standards of service.

2022

Advanced Solutions

Introduced preventive maintenance solutions and ticket resolution system to enhance our service offerings.

2023

Further Expansion

Added Kallakurichi and Chidambaram to our service area and continued to invest in the latest technologies and training.
